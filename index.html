ABOUT US

Welcome to Top Hedge, started in 2024 by Rydge Rivard after leaving his corporate desk job and getting back to his roots in the garden. Rydge has spent more than two years living abroad experiencing different cultures and landscapes. He draws inspiration from these places when designing a space to bring new ideas that work with Norfolk County's climate. Our maintenance team follows the best practices of the legendary gardener Lewis Hill on pruning, trimming, and designing to maintain healthy landscapes.
