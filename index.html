ABOUT US

Welcome to Top Garden, where we transform outdoor spaces into breathtaking landscapes. Founded in 2023 by Rydge Rivard after a friend requested his expertise for a spring cleanup and hedge trimming job. Rydge discovered he loved the satisfaction his work brought, inspiring him to start the business. Our team is committed to creating beautiful spaces that bring out the best of every property we touch.
