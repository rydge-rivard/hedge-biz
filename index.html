ABOUT US

Welcome to Top Hedge, your answer for bringing life and order back into your green spaces. Started in 2024 by Rydge Rivard after leaving his corporate desk job and getting back to his roots in the garden. Rydge comes from a family of gardeners and loves transforming green spaces. Our team follows best practices outlined by the Royal Horticulture Society on pruning, trimming, and design to create beautiful and healthy spaces that bring out the best of every property we touch.
