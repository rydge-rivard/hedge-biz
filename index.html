SERVICES

Take back your weekend.

Lawn Care

Keep your lawn looking its best with our professional lawn mowing and edging services. We'll ensure a perfectly manicured lawn with clean, crisp edges, enhancing your property's curb appeal and creating a beautiful outdoor space you can enjoy.

Mulch Installation

Our mulch delivery and installation services help to complement your landscaping. Mulch not only adds a polished look to your garden beds but also helps retain moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature, contributing to healthier plants and a more beautiful yard.

Tree, Shrub, & Hedge Trimming

We provide professional hedge, shrub, and bush trimming services throughout Norfolk County. Regular trimming not only enhances the beauty and curb appeal of your property, but also promotes healthy growth and prevents overgrowth that can become a nuisance.

Professional Property Cleanup

Typical property cleanups involve weeding, trimming bushes, leaf removal, or spraying gravel areas for weeds. We usually do a spring cleanup for summer readiness and to clear out the winter storm debris. In late fall our cleanups focus on cutting back perennials, gathering fallen leaves, and getting the property ready for winter.

ABOUT US

Welcome to RYD Landscaping, started in 2024 by Rydge Rivard. Rydge grew up in Walsh working different jobs around Norfolk County before studying Business at Brock University. After school, he worked at Dennis' Horseradish in Delhi and most recently spent a summer working with his friends and neighbours on their farm. Rydge loved being outside and working with his hands again which led to the beginnings of RYD Landscaping. Rydge and his team of part-time friends are here to help keep your property looking beautiful and give you back your free time.
